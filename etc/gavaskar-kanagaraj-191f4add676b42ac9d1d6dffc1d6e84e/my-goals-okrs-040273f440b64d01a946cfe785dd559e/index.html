My Goals & OKRs

On this page you can reflect in more detail on your personal learning goals and specify OKRs to make sure you can track your progress.

Remember that you can use the SKATE Competence model and the Bloom Taxonomy to increase the variety of goals and choose explicit language so others and your future your don’t misunderstand what you want to achieve.

You can also invite people to give you feedback on your goals or create small groups to do timed check-ins to talk about your goals and your progress.

Have fun!
